Security

Your security is our priority

We've built SecureSign with the highest security standards to protect your sensitive documents.

Security Features

Encryption
AES-256 encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation and 100,000 iterations for maximum security
Document Storage
Documents are stored as encrypted base64 text in Firebase, ensuring no access to plaintext data
Authentication
Secure Firebase Authentication with support for multi-factor authentication for added security
Compliance
Compliant with eIDAS (EU) and ESIGN Act (US) regulations for legally binding electronic signatures

About

About SecureSign

We're on a mission to make document signing secure, private, and accessible to everyone.

SecureSign was created with a vision to provide end-to-end encrypted document signing that puts privacy and security first. In a world where data breaches are increasingly common, we believe your sensitive documents deserve the highest level of protection.

Our platform combines military-grade encryption with user-friendly interfaces to make secure document signing accessible to individuals and businesses of all sizes. We've built SecureSign to be compliant with international regulations for electronic signatures, ensuring that documents signed on our platform are legally binding.

Whether you're signing a contract, an NDA, or any other important document, SecureSign provides the tools you need to do it securely and confidently.
